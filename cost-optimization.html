Cost Optimization

Healthcare Express Scripts

A multi-year evolution of a prescription cost optimization experience — from a cognitively overwhelming single-page model to a flexible, research-driven switching platform that improved conversions by 320% and lifted positive member sentiment by 10 percentage points.

Challenge

The original experience surfaced all eligible savings opportunities simultaneously on a single page. Members frequently stalled — reviewing options but failing to act — despite clear savings potential. The primary barrier was decision structure, not lack of motivation.

Objectives

Reduce cognitive load and improve conversion through structural simplification. Scale the experience incrementally across new opportunity types. Evolve from feature-based iteration to a flexible platform capable of supporting any prescription change scenario.

Role & Tools

UX Design Advisor — multi-year engagement (2022–2025) spanning discovery research, evaluative usability testing, behavioral analytics, call center feedback analysis, and on-page sentiment surveys.

Three Phases

How the work evolved

01

Phase 1 (2022) — Structural Simplification

The original experience surfaced all savings opportunities on a single page — high cognitive load, competing CTAs, structural rigidity. Discovery research revealed the barrier was decision structure, not motivation. We introduced a multi-step guided experience surfacing one opportunity at a time, each step focused on a single recommendation with scoped messaging and CTAs. Result: a 320% increase in conversion.

Progressive Disclosure Guided Decision-Making 320% Conversion Lift
02

Phase 2 (2023–2024) — Incremental Scaling

New opportunity types were added gradually through an intentional phased rollout — validating each independently, adapting patterns to different mechanics, maintaining stability while expanding breadth. Research focused on step-level funnel performance, behavioral comparisons across user segments, qualitative call center feedback, and edge cases that broke earlier assumptions.

Funnel Analysis Behavioral Analytics Iterative Delivery
03

Phase 3 (2025) — Platform Enablement

Earlier releases struggled to accommodate new or atypical switch types without bespoke logic. The 2025 release introduced a fully flexible decision framework that abstracts switching mechanics from specific business rules — supporting any opportunity type, adapting dynamically to eligibility and pricing, enabling future scenarios without redesign. On-page sentiment surveys captured real-time feedback at critical moments.

Flexible Architecture On-Page Surveys +10pts Positive Sentiment

Measuring Success

On-Page Surveys: Closing the Feedback Loop

With a fundamentally new interaction model in place, historical analytics alone were insufficient. We implemented on-page surveys across critical moments in the switching flow — capturing quantitative sentiment at the moment of decision, qualitative feedback tied to specific steps, and early signals of friction before issues escalated to support. This created a real-time feedback loop connecting user sentiment directly to product decisions.

Key Insight

From Optimization to Enablement

Across the lifecycle of this work, the focus evolved:

  • 2022: Structural simplification to unlock conversion gains
  • 2023–2024: Incremental scaling and validation across programs
  • 2025: Platform enablement to support future growth

The 320% conversion increase resulted from reducing cognitive load. The 10-point sentiment lift resulted from restoring flexibility and user control. Rather than chasing isolated optimizations, this work shows the value of sequenced transformation over time.
